Hello, I am Rui Zou. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Nanjing University in 2016, and my Master's and Ph.D. degrees from Central China Normal University in 2020 and 2024, respectively. After completing my Ph.D., I joined Renmin University of China as a postdoctoral researcher. My primary research interests are artificial intelligence and human-machine collaboration, and the application of large language models in education. My work has been published in conferences and journals such as AAAI, TMM, and ESWA. You can reach me via email at zouruixyz@hotmail.com.
